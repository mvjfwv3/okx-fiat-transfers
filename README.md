## Common Bank Transfer Issues and How to Fix Them

Even on a well-built platform, bank transfers occasionally go sideways. Here are the recurring problems and what to do about them:

**Deposit not showing up.** First, check the processing time for your method — ACH can take up to 2 business days, wire up to 3, international wire up to 5. Bank holidays add delays. If you're past the estimated window, check your Deposit History page for status, then contact OKX support via in-app chat with your reference number and transfer date.

**Transfer returned or rejected.** The usual culprits: name mismatch between your OKX account and bank account, incorrect routing or account numbers, your bank blocking crypto exchange transfers, or exceeding daily/monthly limits. Fix the underlying issue — update your profile details, correct banking information, or contact your bank to authorize the transfer — then retry.

**Name mismatch errors.** Your OKX account name and bank account name must match exactly. Use only personal bank accounts for personal OKX accounts — third-party or business accounts will be rejected. If the problem persists, submit a support ticket with ID and bank document scans.

**Funds on hold after deposit.** ACH deposits may appear instantly but have a brief holding period before they're available for withdrawal. This is a security measure, not a fee. The hold typically clears within a day or two. You can usually trade with the funds immediately even while withdrawal is restricted.

> Pro tip: Ensure your OKX and bank names and addresses match exactly, and always double-check entered bank details before submitting. Most rejected transfers trace back to a typo or a name that doesn't match character-for-character.
